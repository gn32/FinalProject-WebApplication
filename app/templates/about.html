About NJIT

Welcome to New Jersey Institute of Technology. As the state’s public polytechnic university, we continue to invest in the renewal of our existing facilities in addition to adding new spaces, with the goal of providing an exceptional teaching, learning and living environment.

The university’s multidisciplinary curriculum and computing-intensive approach to education provide the technological proficiency, business know-how, and leadership skills that future CEOs and entrepreneurs will need to succeed. With an enrollment of over 11,400 undergraduate and graduate students, NJIT offers small-campus intimacy with the resources of a major public research university.

Enrollment and Employment (2016-2017)

  • Total enrollment: over 11,400
  • SAT average composite score for fall 2017 freshmen is 1286 (top 25%); for Honors College freshmen, the average composite SAT score is 1461 (top 10%)
  • More than 50% of freshman students and 25% overall live on campus (undergraduates)
  • Over 75,000 employees, working in 665 New Jersey organizations, have been trained by NJIT’s Division of Continuing Professional Education since 1990
  • 281 tenured or tenure-track faculty; 98 percent hold doctoral degrees or have the highest degree attainable in their field
  • The student-faculty ratio is 17:1
  • Over 1,300 full-time employees
  • More than 4,000 students and teachers in pre-college educational programs, the pipeline for STEM
  • Over 7,400 prospective students and their parents visit campus annually for open houses and tours

    • Research and Development

  • NJIT’s research expenditures totaled more than $140 million in FY2017.
  • As of 2017, NJIT has 220 U.S. patents and 107 pending U.S. patent applications.
  • VentureLink at NJIT is New Jersey's largest high growth startup incubator. From free resources for student-led startups to sophisticated technology-forward headquarters for mature companies, VentureLink provides the space, mentorship, and resources startups need to grow and thrive. The space is the physical home of startups and innovative entrepreneurship on NJIT's campus. VentureLink is the conduit from student and faculty led ideas to New Jersey's broader technology community. The program hosts seminars, workshops, and competitions that will make any startup successful.
  • Launched in 2014, the New Jersey Innovation Institute (NJII) is a nonprofit corporate entity partnering with industry and government to provide innovative and practical solutions for economic development, technical assistance, joint ventures and commercialization of technology.

    • 2020 Vision - A Strategic Plan

    On July 1, 2015, NJIT introduced 2020 Vision – A Strategic Plan to chart the university’s course during the second decade of the 21st century. It is the product of diverse perspectives, an exceptional breadth of talent and deep concern for all aspects of the university’s mission. The five core strategic priorities are:

  • Students – To support and increase the number of high-achieving students who graduate
  • Learning – To provide a challenging, hands-on and relevant curriculum
  • Scholarly Research – To achieve national and international preeminence and industry engagement
  • Community – To engage locally, regionally and globally
  • Investments – To fund a complete spectrum of resources including human capital and physical infrastructure

    • NJIT will realize this vision through ongoing assessment of its progress to ensure success. This continuous assessment will keep the university on course toward becoming one of the premier polytechnic institutions in the country, renowned for excellence in education, eminence in research, generosity in service and dedication to economic progress. To learn more about our strategic goals, visit 2020 Vision.
